Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the identity of the person contacting you
- Avoid accepting unknown payment requests
- Use strong and unique passwords for accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious activity
- Do not install apps from unknown sources

Financial/UPI Safety Tips:
- Never approve payment requests from unknown sources
- Always double-check UPI IDs before sending money

Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
